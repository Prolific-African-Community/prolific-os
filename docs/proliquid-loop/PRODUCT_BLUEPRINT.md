# Proliquid Product Blueprint

## 1. Product Purpose

Proliquid is a premium financial operating platform for Luxembourg holdings, SPVs, family offices, and investment structures. Its purpose is to combine disciplined human service with a structured digital workspace so that accounting, reporting, documentation, and financial operations can be managed with greater control, visibility, and continuity.

The product exists to help clients:

- establish and manage Luxembourg structures more confidently
- centralize documents, financial records, and reporting outputs
- reduce fragmented follow-up across emails, spreadsheets, and disconnected service providers
- improve traceability across accounting, payments, review, and reporting

What the user sees:

- a secure workspace centered on entities, documents, accounting activity, and reporting status
- clear operational views rather than generic file storage
- structured records, statuses, and review flows

What Proliquid automates:

- organization of source data
- workflow visibility
- draft suggestions where rules are stable
- prioritization and routing of operational work

What remains human-controlled:

- judgment-heavy accounting decisions
- ambiguous classifications
- high-value approvals
- sensitive investor, intercompany, distribution, and investment treatments

## 2. Target Users

The product is designed for clients and operators involved in Luxembourg structure administration.

### Primary client profiles

- entrepreneurs establishing Luxembourg structures
- international investors
- holding companies
- SPVs and private investment vehicles
- family offices
- real estate investment structures
- cross-border groups

### Primary user groups

- client principals and directors
- finance and administration teams
- accountants and controllers
- family office coordinators
- investor-reporting stakeholders
- external or internal operational reviewers

What the user sees:

- a workspace aligned to the structure they are responsible for
- relevant records by role, not an undifferentiated system

What the user can do:

- access entity records
- monitor status
- review financial and documentary items
- participate in approvals and follow-up

What remains human-controlled:

- role-sensitive access
- final sign-off responsibilities
- interpretation of exceptional matters

The product should distinguish clearly between internal control roles and external visibility roles.

### Internal roles

- platform administrator
- organization administrator
- accountant
- reviewer
- operations coordinator

### External roles

- client director or manager
- investor or shareholder viewer

What the user sees:

- a role-appropriate version of the platform
- only the modules, records, and actions relevant to their responsibilities

What the user can do:

- external roles can monitor, upload, review, and access approved outputs depending on entitlement

What remains human-controlled:

## 13. Human Approval Points

Human approval must remain a visible part of the product, not a hidden fallback.

### Core approval points

- accounting proposals with ambiguity
- high-value financial events
- invoice treatments with unclear VAT or capital-versus-expense implications
- bank movements involving funding, investment, intercompany, or distributions
- reconciliations with multiple plausible outcomes
- reporting outputs before release
- investor or client-facing materials where sensitivity is high

What the user sees:

- clear approval status
- responsible reviewer
- items awaiting decision
- reason for escalation where relevant

What Proliquid automates:

- routing to the correct review stage
- visibility of unresolved approvals
- preservation of approval history

What remains human-controlled:

- the decision itself
- the rationale for exceptions or overrides

## 15. Long-Term Product Vision

The long-term vision is for Proliquid to become the operating system through which Luxembourg structures are established, administered, monitored, and reported with institutional-grade discipline.

In that future state:

- clients experience a coherent financial operating environment rather than fragmented service delivery
- service teams work through structured workflows rather than ad hoc reconstruction
- accounting, bank activity, documents, and reporting become part of one controlled operating loop
- AI increases throughput and consistency without replacing accountable human judgment

What the user sees:

- a calm, structured, premium financial platform
- fewer blind spots
- stronger continuity across periods and entities

What Proliquid automates:

- more of the routine preparation layer
- better early detection of issues
- cleaner operational visibility across all modules

What remains human-controlled:

- judgment, accountability, and approval over material financial outcomes
- legal, tax, governance, and investor-sensitive interpretation

## Notes

This blueprint should remain product-oriented, service-aware, and conservative in control design. It should describe a premium Luxembourg financial operating platform where automation improves execution, but human responsibility remains visible and central.
